Important Notes for Dad
The rental security deposit of $1,200 is fully refundable when Joe vacates the unit — it is not a sunk cost to the family.
The bank account deposit of $1,000 is committed by Dad to seed Joe's Singapore account. This is not a gift — Joe will reimburse Dad gradually over time. Singapore banks charge a $5 fee if the account balance falls below $1,000, so this amount must be maintained at all times to avoid unnecessary penalties.
Joe is personally responsible for $2,490 of his own expenses, including food, transport, personal setup, insurance, tax reserve, and emergency buffer — demonstrating strong financial accountability.
The income tax reserve of $140/month is set aside responsibly to ensure Joe is never caught off-guard during Singapore's annual tax filing season in March–April.
The AIA Insurance subscription ensures Joe is covered medically and for life — a responsible long-term commitment that protects both Joe and the family.
